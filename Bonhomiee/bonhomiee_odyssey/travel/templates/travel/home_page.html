How It Works

Our simple 4-step process makes corporate travel management effortless

1

Submit Request

Employees submit travel requests with all necessary details through our platform.

2

Manager Approval

Requests are automatically routed to the appropriate manager for approval.

3

Travel Booking

Once approved, our system books flights, hotels, and transportation.

4

Expense Reporting

Employees submit expenses which are automatically matched to trips.
